Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how YummyCrash ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile game application.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information.

  • Game progress data (level scores, stars, coins, lives, settings) is stored only on your device using local storage. It is never uploaded to our servers.
  • We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, photos, device identifiers, advertising IDs, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • The app does not contain analytics, crash reporting, or any third-party tracking SDKs.

2. How we use information

Local game data is used solely to save your progress on your device so that you can continue playing where you left off. No data leaves your device.

3. Third parties

We do not share data with any third parties. The app does not display advertisements and does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The only third parties involved are Apple and Google, which apply their standard platform usage statistics when you download or update the app from the App Store or Google Play. We do not control or have access to this data.

4. Children's privacy

YummyCrash is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children of any age. Because no personal data is collected from anyone, the game is fully compliant with COPPA, GDPR-K, and applicable children's privacy laws.

5. Your choices

  • You can reset all local game data at any time from Settings → Reset Progress.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data.
  • There is no account to delete because we do not maintain user accounts.

6. Security

Because game data is stored only on your device, its security depends on your device's protections. We never transmit personal information to our servers — there are no servers.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is always available inside the app under Settings → Privacy Policy, and on this page.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at burakerolonline@gmail.com.
